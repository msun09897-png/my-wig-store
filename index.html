— Our Story —

A craft, not a trend.

LUMIÈRE was born from a simple belief: that a wig should not feel like a wig. It should feel like the hair you were always meant to have.

Each of our pieces is hand-tied by artisans with decades of experience. We source only virgin human hair — never blended, never chemically processed — and finish every wig with HD Swiss lace that vanishes against the skin.

From our atelier to your doorstep, we ship worldwide with care, discretion, and a 30-day promise of satisfaction.
